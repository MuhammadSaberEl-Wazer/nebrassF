About us

Believing that one should choose the right and reliable person before studying with him and learning from him; We are:
  • - A group of graduates from Al-Azhar University in Egypt, the largest and oldest university in the world for teaching Islamic and Arabic sciences.
  • - We learned from many reliable and respected scholars in the field of Islamic and Arab sciences, and of course, the Holy Qur’an.
